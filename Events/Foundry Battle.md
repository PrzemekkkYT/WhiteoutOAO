### Info before the fight
```
The main targets are
-> Transit Station (-50% teleport cooldown)
-> Boiler Room (-50% control time)
The rest is up to you
TIPS:
-> Remember that you have free marching speedups every 5 minutes.
-> If you lost too many troops, you can leave the battle and rejoin after 12 minutes. (Click on timer, and there Leave Battlefield)
-> If opponent holds important building with his strongest heroes, try to attack his city
```
